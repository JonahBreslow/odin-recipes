Grilled Chicken & Veggie Skewers

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers (pre-soaked in water to prevent burning)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well to make the marinade.
  2. Place the chicken cubes in a separate bowl and pour half of the marinade over them. Toss the chicken to ensure even coating, then cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. While the chicken is marinating, prepare the vegetables. Thread the marinated chicken, bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes alternately onto the pre-soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Brush the grill grates with a bit of oil to prevent sticking. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. While grilling, baste the skewers with the remaining marinade to enhance the flavors.
  7. Once cooked, remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Nutrition (yields 4 servings)

  • Fats: 12.0g
